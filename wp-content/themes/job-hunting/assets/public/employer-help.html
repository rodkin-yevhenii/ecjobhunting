Welcome to the Employer Help Center!

How do I post a job for free?

We provide you with a four day trial of есjobhunting, on us. You will be able to post a job ad and keep whatever candidates you receive.

Step 1: On the upper right corner of the есjobhunting homepage, click on “Post a Job.” Select any plan and enter a credit card to validate your account. (If you decide to cancel before the free trial ends, you will not be charged anything.)

Step 2: Complete your job ad browse candidates.

Since 2019, ecjobhunting.com has given job seekers free access to millions of jobs from thousands of company websites and job boards.
